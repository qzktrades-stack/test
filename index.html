FAQ

What is a bookmarklet?

A tiny piece of JavaScript saved as a bookmark. Clicking it runs the code on the page you are viewing. It's safe, portable and works on most browsers without installing an extension.

Is Axiom Tools free?

Yes. The core toolkit is free. Advanced pro modules can be added later, but the basic features shown here will remain free.

Does it collect my data?

No. The bookmarklet runs entirely in your browser (local-first). It does not upload your private keys, orders, or account data.
